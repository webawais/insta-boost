Frequently Asked Questions

How does InstaBoost work?

InstaBoost uses Instagram's official API to safely grow your account by connecting you with real users in your niche. We never use bots or fake accounts.

Is my account safe?

Absolutely. We use read-only access and never store your password. Our methods comply with Instagram's terms of service.

How long does it take to see results?

Most users see increased engagement within 24-48 hours. Growth continues gradually to appear natural and avoid detection.

Can I target specific audiences?

Yes! In the boost settings you can specify hashtags, locations, and competitor accounts to target relevant users.
